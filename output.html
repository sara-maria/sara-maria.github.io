I could not believe my luck when I learned about Biota Beats. This project brings together art, science, and the communication of ideas through visuals. On top of that, it is all about the microbiome! Very happy to be involved with the amazing, multidisciplinary Street Bio team at EMW in Cambridge, USA.

For the Biota Beats project, I worked on the question: "How to best visualize audio?" Different variations of music equalizers were explored. One of them was a circular one.
